G&S is a seasoned leader in Occupational Health and Safety, with over 8 years of experience helping businesses create safer, more productive work environments. We specialize in designing, implementing, and updating Occupational Health and Safety Management Systems tailored to each client's unique needs. Our services include comprehensive Safe Work at Heights training, thorough assessments of workplace psychological risks, and the development of customized intervention plans. Our Services

Psychosocial Risk Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation of workplace factors that may affect employee mental health and wellbeing, identifying potential risks and areas for improvement.

Psychosocial Analysis of the Workplace

Detailed examination of workplace dynamics, organizational culture, and interpersonal relationships to optimize work environment and employee satisfaction.

Design of a Psychosocial Risk Factor Intervention Plan

Custom-tailored strategies and action plans to address identified psychosocial risks, including implementation guidelines and monitoring protocols.

Safe Work at Heights

Specialized training and certification for working at elevated positions, ensuring compliance with safety regulations and best practices for fall protection.

Design and updating the Occupational Safety and Health Management System

Development and maintenance of comprehensive safety management systems aligned with international standards and local regulations.
